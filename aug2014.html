Business Relevance

In this half-day event, there are talks and interactive sessions with real life examples that will help you inculcate, understand, and measure Business Relevance in your team/project. The topics will vary from how business relevance can be used to measure ROI in different domains to infusing business relevance in our teams' mindset to environment and social relevance.
